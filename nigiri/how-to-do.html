1



With your hand wet, grab about 20 grams of shari rice, and shape it to a long, oval form.


2



Keep the raw fish in the fridge right up to the point when you're about to prepare it.


3



Make sure both your kitchen and your hands are cool (run your hands under cold water first if necessary).


4



Work quickly and, once it is cut, return the fish to the fridge immediately.


5



place the slice of fish, with the wasabi side facing down, on the rice. Lay it gently on the rice, and then press it firmly to stick with the rice. You might want to use the other hand to hold the nigiri sushi from the sides while pressing it from above, to avoid “rice loss”.


6




Eat within 4 hours.
